Enemies
Evil Sword
"Lunges towards you but fast and scary."
Mechanics
- After 1 - 1.5 seconds, Evil Sword will face towards the nearest player creating a line. It will lunge following this line instantly killing on contact
- Evil sword can only spawn after level 10 and will only appear after choosing sword
- Evil sword has a limit of 2 permanant Evil swords, and a cap of 10 temporary Evil Swords
